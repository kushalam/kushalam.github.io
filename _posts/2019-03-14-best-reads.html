An assortment of some excellent papers I have read over the years

Updated: March 2018

On Dynamics and Control

• Ljung, Lennart. “Perspectives on System Identification.” Annual Reviews in Control 34, no. 1 (April 2010): 1–12. https://doi.org/10.1016/j.arcontrol.2009.12.001.

• Neculai, Andrei. “Modern Control Theory. A Historical Perspective.” Romania: Research Institute for Informatics, 2005.

• Vamvoudakis, Kyriakos G., Panos J. Antsaklis, Warren E. Dixon, Joao P. Hespanha, Frank L. Lewis, Hamidreza Modares, and Bahare Kiumarsi. “Autonomy and Machine Intelligence in Complex Systems: A Tutorial.” In 2015 American Control Conference (ACC), 5062–79. Chicago, IL, USA: IEEE, 2015. https://doi.org/10.1109/ACC.2015.7172127.

On Math and Optimization

• Jones, Donald R. “A Taxonomy of Global Optimization Methods Based on Response Surfaces.” Journal of Global Optimization 21, no. 4 (December 2001): 345–83. https://doi.org/10.1023/A:1012771025575.

• Martins, Joaquim R. R. A., and Andrew B. Lambe. “Multidisciplinary Design Optimization: A Survey of Architectures.” AIAA Journal 51, no. 9 (September 2013): 2049–75. https://doi.org/10.2514/1.J051895.

• Powell, Warren B. “What You Should Know about Approximate Dynamic Programming.” Naval Research Logistics 56, no. 3 (April 2009): 239–49. https://doi.org/10.1002/nav.20347.

• Powell, Warren B. "A unified framework for stochastic optimization." European Journal of Operational Research, Volume 275, Issue 3, (June 2019): 795-821.

• Viana, Felipe A. C., Timothy W. Simpson, Vladimir Balabanov, and Vasilli Toropov. “Special Section on Multidisciplinary Design Optimization: Metamodeling in Multidisciplinary Design Optimization: How Far Have We Really Come?” AIAA Journal 52, no. 4 (April 2014): 670–90. https://doi.org/10.2514/1.J052375.
