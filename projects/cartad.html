Constraint Analysis of a Transport Aircraft using Optimization

In this project, I conducted the contraint analysis procedure by coding the various aircraft performance constraints as relationships between thrust-to-weight (T/W) and wing loading (W/S) and obtained the required values that result in optimum value of important design parameters such as Maximum Take-Off Weight, Total Fuel Weight, or Direct Operating Cost, using Simulated Annealing optimization algorithm. I demonstrated with a case study for constraint analysis for a 100-seater twin-turbofan regional transport aircraft for three objective functions of minimization of total take-off weight, minimization of total fuel carried, and minimization of direct operating costs. The optimum MTOW and DOC solutions were found to be almost identical, and the Take-off and Landing Constraints were seen to be the most critical.
