Aircraft Design

In the study, I developed a program to carry out the constraint analysis for a transport aircraft computationally. I encoded the relationships between the requirements in a single constraint analysis module. In order to obtain the optimum results, I linked the entire procedure to SIMANN Simulated Annealing optimization algorithm. For optimization, I considered three objective functions, viz., Max. Take Off Weight (MTOW), Total Fuel Weight (TFW), and Direct Operating Cost (DOC).
